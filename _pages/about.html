I have worked in the government sector for 21 years as part of a 25-year career in Information Technology. My roles have included customer support, cybersecurity, technical communication, and knowledge management. This long-term experience has given me a solid understanding of how IT operations function within government and their private-sector partners.

In addition to my technical knowledge, I have cross-training and experience in project management and logistics. I use this background to help technical teams and other departments understand each other better. By applying skills from outside the IT field, I help solve problems and keep projects moving forward efficiently.

Outside of work, I am an active artist with experience in many different forms. I work across a multitude of mediums from digital media like video editing, 3D modeling, and photo editing, as well as hands-on crafts like lapidary, ceramics, jewelry making, sewing, and prop creation. This mix of technical work and artistic practice helps me approach my job with both logic and creativity.
